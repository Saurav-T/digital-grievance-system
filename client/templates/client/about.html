Digital Government Platform · Nepal

About the Digital Grievance
and Notice Board System

Connecting citizens and government through transparent,
efficient, and accessible digital public services.

This platform enables every citizen to submit grievances online and access official government notices all in one centralised location, without visiting a government office or navigating multiple websites. It bridges the communication gap between the public and government institutions through a single, secure digital window.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve communication between citizens and government by providing a secure, transparent, and user-friendly digital platform for grievance management and official notice publication. We believe every citizen deserves timely responses and easy access to information that affects their daily lives.

What We Do

📝

Online Grievance Submission

Submit complaints and suggestions anytime without visiting offices.

📢

Official Notice Publication

Receive authentic notices published directly by government authorities.

📊

Transparent Tracking

Track the progress and status of submitted grievances in real time.

🔒

Secure Digital Services

Protect citizen information through secure authentication and data handling.

Objectives

  • Improve accessibility to government services.
  • Reduce paperwork and manual processing.
  • Enhance transparency and accountability.
  • Ensure timely grievance resolution.
  • Deliver official notices digitally.
  • Promote citizen participation.
